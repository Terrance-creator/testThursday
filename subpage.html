Born July 20, 1999 Bashar Barakah Jackson, also known as Pop Smoke, was well known throughout the music industry for his songwriting abilities and deep baritone voice. In April 2019, he released his debut mixtape "Meet the Woo" collaborating with many artists including superstar Nicki Minaj. February 2020, Pop Smoke released his highly anticipated follow up mixtape "Meet the Woo 2", featuring Quavo and A Boogie wit da Hoodie. On February 19, 2020, Pop Smoke was fatally shot during a home invasion at his LA residence.
